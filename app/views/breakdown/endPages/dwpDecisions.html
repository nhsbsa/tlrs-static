Challenge a Penalty Charge Notice

We need proof that you were named on a claim for Income Support between 1 November and 30 November 2017

Your certifcate was not valid at the time you claimed. This may have been because your certificate had expired or you hadn't got the certificate in time. But if you can prove that you were named on a claim for Income Support, you won’t have to pay anything.

You now have 14 days from today to send us your proof.

What to do next

Choose one of the following options:

  • Send us proof that you were named on a claim for Income Support at the time you claimed free or reduced cost dental treatment. If you send us valid proof, you won’t have to pay anything.
  • Pay the total amount of £68.60.

If you were not entitled to free dental treatment but have an exceptional reason why you made the claim, you can ask us to review your Penalty Charge Notice. Find out if you can ask for a review.
