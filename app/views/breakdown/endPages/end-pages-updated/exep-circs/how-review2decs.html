Challenge a Penalty Charge Notice

Asking for a Penalty Charge Notice review

What you need to do

You need to contact us and explain the exceptional reason why you claimed free or reduced cost dental treatment.

How to contact us

When you contact us you must also include your:

  • full name
  • date of birth
  • Penalty Charge Notice reference number 7489202837
Online

Use our online contact form

In the subject box select ‘Dental penalty charge.’
We reply within 2 working days.
Email Email us at: nhsbsa.decs@nhsbsa.nhs.uk

We reply within 2 working days.
Telephone

Call us on: 0300 330 1293

Lines are available Monday to Friday from 8am to 6pm and Saturday from 9am to 3pm, UK time

If you're deaf or hard of hearing and you have a textphone (sometimes called a minicom), you can contact us using the Text Relay Service.
Dial 18001 and then 0300 330 1293.

Post

Write to us at:

Dental Penalty Charges Team,
NHSBSA,
Bridge House,
152 Pilgrim Street,
Newcastle Upon Tyne,
NE1 6SN

We offer a telephone translation service and provide documents in large print and braille on request.

What happens next?

If the review is successful

Your penalty charge will be reduced or removed. However you will still need to pay the original dental treatment charge.

If the review is unsuccessful

You will need to pay both the penalty charge and the original dental treatment charge.
