Challenge an enquiry letter

Who can ask for a review?

We understand that you may have made an honest mistake and didn’t mean to do anything wrong.

If you have an exceptional reason why you claimed free or reduced dental treatment that you weren't entitled to, you can ask for a review.

Reasons we won’t usually accept

We won't usually review your enquiry letter if:

  • your exemption certificate had expired
  • you feel that you were misadvised by your GP or by pharmacy staff
  • you thought that your benefit entitled you to claim
  • you thought your prescription prepayment certificate would automatically renew

What to do next

If you have an exceptional reason why you made the claim at the time, you can contact us to review your enquiry letter.

If you’re not entitled to a review

You will be sent a penalty charge notice.
