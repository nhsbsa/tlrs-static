How to avoid a future Penalty Charge Notice

  • Check the from and until dates to ensure your HC3 certificate is valid at the time of your treatments
  • Check the HC3 contribution amount on your HC3 certificate. If the dental treatment charge is higher, you must pay the HC3 contribution amount
  • Make sure your name and address are correct at your dental practice and on your NHS exemption

If you were not entitled to free or reduced cost dental treatment but have an exceptional reason why you made the claim, you can ask us to review your Penalty Charge Notice. Find out if you can ask for a review.
