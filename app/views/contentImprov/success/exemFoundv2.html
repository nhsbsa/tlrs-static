Why did I receive a Penalty Charge Notice?

The details on your exemption certificate do not match those taken from your prescription. Checks for exemptions are done automatically using these details from your prescription:

  • Name
  • Address
  • Date of birth

    • One or more of the above details on your certificate may be different to the details on your prescription.

  • NHS number

    Your NHS number may have been missing from your prescription.

Make sure these are correct on your NHS exemption certificate, and at your GP surgery, dental practice, and any benefits organisations. If they’re not, you might get another Penalty Charge Notice.

How to check these details are correct on your NHS exemption

For medical and maternity exemption certificates, and prescription prepayment certificates

  • Call us on 0300 330 1341

For NHS tax credit exemption certificates

  • Call us on 0300 330 1347

For HC2 and HC3 certificates

  • Call us on 0300 330 1343

Lines are available Monday to Friday from 8am to 6pm and Saturday from 9am to 3pm (Your call will be charged at a local rate).
